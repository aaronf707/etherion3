**Join the decentralized future with Etherion Network (ETR).**

Etherion Network is a next-generation mobile mining node designed to establish a sustainable, community-driven digital economy. Built with cutting-edge technology and powered by real-time data, Etherion allows you to contribute to network security while accumulating ETR tokens directly from your mobile device.

*   **Hardware Depot**: Don't just wait—grow. Spend your earned ETR to upgrade your mining rig. Progress from basic CPUs to flagship GPUs like the RTX 4090, and ultimately to industrial-grade Enterprise ASIC nodes.

*   **Proof of Mining**: Our unique 4-hour session cycle rewards active, human-verified participation, ensuring a fair distribution for all real users.
*   **High-Tech Interface**: Choose your style. Toggle between a classic hacker-style terminal CLI and a sleek, modern Material 3 graphical dashboard.

*   **Team Growth**: Build your mining farm. Every active referral gives you a permanent **+5% hashrate boost**.

*   **Node Progression**: Level up your node to unlock elite hardware and higher session caps.

**🛡️ PRIVACY & SECURITY**

Etherion Network is built on a foundation of transparency.

## 🛡️ Data Safety Form Guide

When filling out the "Data Safety" section in the Play Console, use these answers:

1.  **Does your app collect or share any of the required user data types?** -> **Yes**
2.  **Is all of the user data collected by your app encrypted in transit?** -> **Yes**
3.  **Do you provide a way for users to request that their data be deleted?** -> **Yes**

**Data Types Collected:**
*   **Location**: Precise Location (Required for IP-based yield verification and AdMob).
*   **Personal Info**: Name and Email address (For account creation/sync).
*   **App Activity**: App interactions (For mining actions).
*   **Device or other IDs**: Device IDs (Required for AdMob).
