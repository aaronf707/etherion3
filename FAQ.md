# Etherion Network - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Welcome to the Etherion Network FAQ. This document provides answers to common questions about the ecosystem, mining mechanics, and tokenomics.

---

### 🚀 GENERAL QUESTIONS

#### **What is Etherion Network?**
Etherion Network is a decentralized mobile mining ecosystem that allows users to establish their own mining nodes directly on their mobile devices. It combines a realistic mining simulation with a value-backed token economy.

#### **What sets Etherion apart from other mobile mining apps (like Pi)?**
Etherion Network is built on **Economic Transparency** and **Real-World Backing**. While many projects offer speculative tokens with no clear utility, ETR is different because:
1.  **Revenue Backing**: 75% of all ecosystem revenue (from ads and data research) is used to provide a liquidity floor and backing for the token.
2.  **Hardware Utility**: Users can spend their ETR to upgrade virtual hardware (GPUs/ASICs), which directly increases their production capacity.
3.  **Deflationary Mechanics**: We implement a 1.5% daily emission decay and periodic token burns to protect long-term scarcity.
4.  **Realistic Simulation**: Our live terminal provides a genuine mining experience, including stratum handshakes and share verification, making it a true "Node Simulator."

#### **Is this real mining?**
Etherion uses a "Proof of Participation" model. While it simulates the technical aspects of mining (stratum connections, share acceptance, hashrate), the actual rewards are distributed based on active node engagement and network growth, backed by real-world ecosystem revenue.

#### **What is ETR?**
ETR is the native utility token of the Etherion Network. It has a fixed supply and is used for hardware upgrades, node customization, and as a representation of your contribution to the network.

#### **Is ETR secure?**
Yes. ETR is built on a foundation of security and transparency. User identity and balances are protected by **Google Firebase Authentication** and industrial-grade cloud encryption. Furthermore, ETR has a fixed supply and is backed by **75% of ecosystem revenue**, creating a stable economic floor. We also employ automated anti-fraud systems to prevent bot manipulation and ensure a fair environment for all node operators.

### ⛏️ MINING & HASHRATE

#### **How do I start mining?**
Simply navigate to the Node Dashboard and click "Engage Miner." You may be required to watch a short briefing (ad) to initialize the 4-hour mining session.

#### **Why did my session stop?**
Mining sessions are processed in 4-hour cycles to ensure network security and human verification. Once a session ends, you must manually re-engage the miner to start a new cycle.

#### **What determines my hashrate?**
Your hashrate is influenced by:
*   **Hardware Tier**: Better equipment (GPUs/ASICs) provides a higher base rate.
*   **Team Boost**: +5% bonus for every active referral in your team.
*   **Consistency**: A daily mining streak provides an increasing loyalty bonus.
*   **Region**: Your mining yield is optimized based on your node's geographic location.
*   **Boosts**: Temporary 1.5x overclock boosts are available via the dashboard.

#### **How do I increase my level?**
Your Node Level increases automatically as you accumulate more ETR. Higher levels unlock advanced hardware tiers in the Hardware Depot.

### 💎 TOKENOMICS & VALUE

#### **What is the value of ETR?**
The value of ETR is driven by real-world ecosystem utility and revenue. Unlike traditional speculative tokens, ETR is backed by 75% of all revenue generated through the platform (advertisements and Anonymous Data Research). This revenue is used to provide liquidity, deflationary burn mechanisms, and project stability.

#### **Can I buy ETR?**
Currently, ETR cannot be purchased with traditional currency (fiat). It must be earned through active participation in the network via mining and referrals. This ensures a fair and decentralized distribution of the token during the Accumulation Phase. Any official opportunities to purchase ETR will be announced through our verified channels as we approach the Mainnet launch in Q4 2026.

#### **Is ETR listed on any exchanges?**
ETR is currently in its **Accumulation Phase** and is not yet listed on public exchanges. The official Mainnet launch and exchange listings are projected for **Q4 2026**.

#### **What is the total supply of ETR?**
The maximum supply is hard-capped at **21,000,000 ETR**.

#### **What is the "Burn" mechanism?**
A portion of every transaction and a percentage of global production is periodically "burned" (removed from circulation) to create deflationary pressure.

### 🛡️ PRIVACY & SECURITY

#### **What data do you collect?**
Etherion Network is built with a "Privacy-First" philosophy. We collect necessary authentication data via Firebase and anonymous performance metrics. Participation in the Data Research SDK is strictly optional.

#### **Do I have to complete KYC?**
Currently, **No**. During the Accumulation Phase, you do not need to provide ID to mine. However, KYC may be required during the Mainnet migration in **Q4 2026** for withdrawal eligibility.

#### **Why do some avatars cost ETR?**
Exclusive avatars represent node "Prestige" within the network. Standard icons are free, while specialized and elite icons are purchased using earned ETR. This acts as a "Token Sink," removing ETR from circulation to help maintain the token's long-term value and scarcity.

#### **Can I mine on multiple devices with the same account?**
To maintain network integrity and fair distribution, each node is limited to one active session per device. Attempting to mine on multiple devices with the same account or using automated scripts is a violation of our Anti-Fraud Policy and may result in a permanent node suspension.

#### **How does the Data Research SDK boost my hashrate?**
By opting into the Anonymous Data Research SDK in your settings, you contribute processing power to secure telemetry and network analysis. In exchange for this contribution, your node receives an immediate, permanent **+25% hashrate boost**.
