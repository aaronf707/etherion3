# Etherion Network - Privacy Policy

**System Status: Online | Node v1.0.4-beta**

Etherion Network is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle data to ensure a secure and transparent mining experience.

### 1. Anonymous Data Collection
If opted-in via settings, our app utilizes an Anonymous Data SDK to gather non-personally identifiable information. This data is used solely for network optimization and research purposes. Users who participate receive a +25% hashrate boost.

### 2. Firebase & Authentication
We use Firebase for secure authentication and cloud synchronization of your mining progress. Your email is used strictly for account recovery and node identification within the leaderboards.

### 3. AdMob
Our app displays advertisements via Google AdMob to fund the ETR ecosystem. These ads may collect device identifiers and precise location data to provide relevant content and verify geographic mining yields.

### 4. Account Deletion & Data Removal
We respect your right to control your data. 
*   **How to Delete**: You can request full account deletion by emailing **support@etherion.network** with your User ID or registered email.
*   **Data Processed**: Upon request, all associated mining balances, team history, and personal identifiers will be permanently wiped from our Firebase servers within 48 hours.

### 5. Contact
For support or data requests, contact us at: **support@etherion.network**
