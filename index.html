Privacy Policy

Etherion Network is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle data.

1. Anonymous Data Collection

If opted-in via settings, our app utilizes an Anonymous Data SDK to gather non-personally identifiable information. This data is used solely for network optimization and research purposes. Users who participate receive a +25% hashrate boost.

2. Firebase & Authentication

We use Firebase for secure authentication and cloud synchronization of your mining progress. Your email is used only for account recovery and identification within the leaderboards.

3. AdMob

Our app displays advertisements via Google AdMob to fund the ETR ecosystem. These ads may collect device identifiers to provide relevant content.

4. Contact

For support or data requests, contact us at: support@etherion.network
